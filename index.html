About

After building my first React app, I became deeply interested in software development and decided to make a career change. Previously, I enjoyed a 6-year career at Pacific Gas and Electric Company, a large utility company, where I developed a love for engineering. Encouraged by friends in the tech industry, I began learning programming through online courses. My first project, a Covid-19 tracker app, sparked my passion for coding and problem-solving.

I graduated from the Springboard Coding Bootcamp, where I gained a comprehensive foundation in both frontend and backend development. This experience equipped me with modern skills, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, Express, Python, SQL, React, Redux, and more. I thrive in creative and collaborative environments, continuously seeking to learn new technologies to enhance my expertise.

Currently, I work as a software engineer at Leica Geosystems, focusing on React, TypeScript, and JavaScript, Node.js to build dynamic, high-performance applications. I’ve contributed to optimizing complex UI components, improving performance, and enhancing user experiences.

With a strong foundation in software engineering and experience from the utility industry, I’m excited to bring my skills to a high-functioning, passionate development team. My goal is to keep pushing myself to grow as a software engineer while making meaningful contributions to innovative projects.
